Team AVI

We choose our team after a series of rounds and as such we make sure that only the best of the best candidates are selected. Our team is the backbone of our organization which has made what we are today
and we don't compromise on the quality of our faculty, infrastructure facilitators and administrative staff. We possess professional acumen and domain expertise in our management team as well as training team.

TRAINERS

We give utmost importance to our trainers as they play a major role in imparting the knowledge to the learners. We have a team of highly qualified, certified and experienced trainers who have been continuously thriving for imparting quality education. All of our faculty members are professionals having a Master's degree and with a minimum of 8 years of real time experience in their field and with atleast 3 – 5 years of teaching experience in a specific technology. The selection process comprises of a series of interviews and teaching demonstrations.

ADMINISTRATIVE STAFF

Our administrative staff comprises of Training Administrators, Course Administrators and so on. These administrative teams understand the needs and requirements of the learners and plan the schedule of the course accordingly. They will be the first point of contact for any administrative related issues throughout your association with us.

FACILITIES

AVI Tech training provides top notch online training facilities for the learners. These include sophisticated technologies like web conferencing facilities, latest communication tools in communicating with each other and so on.
